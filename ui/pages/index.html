Why This Exists

What makes it different

Self-custody identity

Your Ed25519 keypair IS your identity AND your Solana wallet address. No accounts, no passwords, no home servers. Back it up with a BIP39 seed phrase.

Encrypted by default

DMs use ECDH + AES-256-GCM end-to-end encryption. The server never sees plaintext. No IP logging, no analytics, no tracking.

Federated

Run your own server. Profiles replicate across the network. Your identity works everywhere. No central authority controls access.

Local-first

Works 100% offline from first launch. The desktop app bundles everything locally. Background sync pulls updates when connected.

3D game engine

Rust/wgpu renderer with planets, voxel asteroids, physics, farming, crafting, quests, and a day/night cycle. All game data is in external files, fully moddable.

Public domain (CC0)

The entire codebase, all assets, all game data. No permission needed, no attribution required. This belongs to everyone, forever.

The Mission

Poverty is lack of capability

Not just lack of money. People trapped in systems they can't understand, knowledge they can't access, skills they never learned.

In 2017, Michael Boisson nearly died. That experience stripped away the noise and left one clear answer: help people become capable of helping themselves. Education is the foundation. Tools are the path. Community is the engine.

Everything here is public domain, CC0. No permission needed. This belongs to everyone.
