# HumanityOS (HOS)

**We are building a game + toolset to help people become more capable, together.**

---

## Explain Like I'm 5

Imagine the world is a giant spaceship.

- Some people know how to grow food.
- Some know how to fix machines.
- Some know how to teach.
- Some know how to organize.

If we share these skills, everyone does better.

**That is our mission:**
Help people learn useful life skills, cooperate at scale, and build systems that reduce suffering.

Not by handouts forever.
By making capability easy to learn and use.

---

## What We're Trying to Accomplish for Humanity

1. **Teach practical skills** (food, building, systems, teamwork)
2. **Make collaboration simple** (chat, shared planning, shared knowledge)
3. **Build resilient communities** (local + global, online + offline)
4. **Prepare for long-term civilization growth** (Earth first, then beyond)

---

## Where the Project Is Headed

We are shaping the platform around five core experiences:

- **Private** — your personal progression (profile, inventory, skills, equipment, quests, logbook)
- **H Dashboard** — your command center and next best actions
- **Public** — shared systems (network, systems, maps, market, learn, knowledge, streams)
- **Ops** — health, deploy, logs, moderation
- **Utility** — search, settings, data, alerts, account

## Why This Exists

Poverty is not only about money.
It is also about missing skills, missing tools, and missing support.

So we build:

- open systems,
- open knowledge,
- practical learning paths,
- and shared infrastructure.

Everything is aimed at helping people become capable of helping themselves and each other.

---

## Project Universe

Project Universe is the long-term simulation/game layer:

- players as crew in humanity's first interstellar fleet,
- non-combat systems first,
- real-world skill transfer through gameplay,
- deep cooperation through infrastructure and missions.
