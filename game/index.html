🤝 Social System

Viewers️ Follow

One-way — shows you care. Use /follow <name>. Right-click a user for quick follow.

🤝 Friends (Mutual Follow)

When both users follow each other, they become friends. This unlocks DMs and shows the 🤝 badge.

🚫 Block

Client-side — hides their messages from your view. Use /block <name>.

Duration️ Rate Limits

Messages: Fibonacci rate limiting (1,1,2,3,5,8… second delays on rapid posting)
New accounts: 5s minimum delay for first 10 minutes
Uploads: 2/minute, 4 image max per user (FIFO — oldest replaced)
API: 10 req/s general, 5 req/s for read endpoints

🏗️ Architecture

🔑 Identity

Ed25519 key pairs stored in your browser — no accounts, no passwords

🔏 Encryption

Messages signed cryptographically — tamper-proof

📡 Communication

WebSocket relay for group chat, WebRTC P2P for voice/video

💾 Storage

SQLite on server, localStorage + IndexedDB on client

🌐 Federation

Servers can link together — your identity works everywhere

Tech stack:

RustAxumTokio SQLiteEd25519BLAKE3 WebRTCVanilla JS

Future: CBOR signed objects, P2P direct messaging, peer-assisted streaming
