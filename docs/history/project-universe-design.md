# Project Universe: Original Design Document (2019-2023)

Extracted from the original Google Docs design documents used during
the Unity development era. Preserved here for reference as HumanityOS
continues the same mission with a new technology stack.

## The Fleet

The game is set aboard an interstellar colony fleet traveling toward
the galaxy's center. Key vessel types:

- **Mothership/Generation Ship** (max 20km length)
- **Biome Ship** (max 2km, ecosystem preservation)
- **Combat Ships** (defense, not primary focus)
- **Faction/Guild Ships** (community-run vessels)

## Economy System

Three currency types:
- **Money**: Generic currency for material goods and equipment
- **Reputation**: Used for account/character upgrades (cybernetics, genetic mods, crafting recipes, permissions)
- **Resources**: Physical goods from farming, mining, salvage, crafting

Markets:
- **Global Market**: Fleet-wide supply/demand for repairs, upgrades, quests
- **Private Market**: Individual, friend, or guild trading

Storage:
- **Home Storage**: Personal with permission-based access
- **Guild Storage**: Upgradeable based on performance
- **Fleet Storage**: Capacity limited by fleet size

## Equipment & Vehicles

- **Walking Mechs**: Max 20m, 200 tons, piloted from internal chamber
- **Wheeled/Treaded/Hover**: Max 6m, 100 tons, traditional vehicles
- **Exosuits/Elementals**: Max 3m, worn like armor
- **Space Suits**: Bulky EVA specialized wear

Weapons: Handheld (pistols, rifles, shotguns, launchers), Exosuit-mounted
(artillery-scale), Vehicle-mounted (unlimited variety within reason)

## Content Earning Methods

- **Farming**: Food and renewables, crop quality affects fleet morale
- **Asteroid Harvesting**: Periodic element collection (water, iron, carbon, platinum)
- **Crafting**: Resource conversion into equipment (higher rewards than raw sales)
- **Quests**: Fleet-requested special resource gathering
- **Missions**: Short procedurally generated adventures
- **Expeditions**: Medium-scale multi-player events
- **Fleet Events**: Server-wide scenarios (alien invasions affecting facilities)

## Death & Respawn

- PvP/PvE Training: Nothing lost
- Live Expeditions: Equipment lost on team wipe
- Emergency Recovery Team options planned

## Character System

- Player-created biography expandable through gameplay
- Autobiography entries for significant events
- Behavior tracked on "rap sheet" affecting NPC trust
- Modular equipment painting for visual customization
- Minimal NPCs (shopkeepers, auction operators, general populace)

## Development Acts

- **Act I**: Colony ship, base mechanics (movement, combat, vehicles), PvP
- **Act II**: Vehicle and AI development, PvE introduction
- **Act III**: Equipment, vehicle, weapon refinement
- **Act IV**: Event systems, hostile threat response, space combat
- **Act V**: TBD based on progress

## Lore

Early 2000s apocalypse killed half of humanity. Subsequent recovery
established a cooperation-focused civilization. Rapid technological
advancement throughout the millennium. Space colonization of Moon,
Mars, and the solar system. Accidental faster-than-light travel
discovery attracted alien visitors. Formation of "Spiral Affinity"
alliance. Construction of massive colony fleet toward galaxy center.

## Technical Systems (Unity Era)

Systems built by Patrick McGuinness:
- **Power System**: Generators, routing stations, substations, breakers
- **Bitmap Interpreter**: Color-coded pixel maps converted to 3D ship layouts
- **Atmospheric Volume System**: Per-room oxygen, pressure, temperature
- **Gas Transfer System**: Pipes distribute oxygen via dual-pressure calculations
- **Crafting System**: Ore processing through finished products
- **Material Library**: Shader-based asset consistency
- **The Mall**: Central hub with stores and NPCs

## SPC Structure

Shaostoul Social Purpose Corporation, registered Washington Dec 2019.
Hybrid for-profit/nonprofit model. Benefits: healthcare, housing, food,
utilities, education for dedicated contributors (~6 months commitment).
