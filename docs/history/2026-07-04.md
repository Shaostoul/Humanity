## The operator's stair-step catch becomes a root-cause chain

The operator spotted stair-stepped tiles in the Humanity page snapshot. Two real
layers under it:

- **v0.684.0** — `draw_stat_card` inherited its parent's layout direction (labels
  and values landed side-by-side at drifting heights in any left-to-right
  container). The widget now owns its internal layout — the universal-widget
  rule, protecting every page that uses it. Humanity's scoreboard also moved to
  the same top-aligned Grid the Civilization page uses.
- **v0.684.3** — the deeper cause: theme.ron's spacing scale was CRUSHED
  (spacing_xs 0.0, sm 0.60 px, md 2.27 — slider-drag/scale-mishap values).
  Every `add_space()` in the app rendered as nothing; governance rows fused into
  "OPENlocal…workshopcloses". Restored to a dense-but-legible scale (2/6/10/14/18,
  card 6), theme.css regenerated from the same source, all 29 snapshots
  re-rendered.
