## v0.678.0 — Stage 1 review fixes (save-authoritative vehicles)

The v0.677.0 pre-commit review had been cut off by the spend limit with three
findings unverified. Adjudicated by hand: two real, one root cause —
`apply_save_to_world` treated vehicles ADDITIVELY while treating inventory as
authoritative, and it is NOT startup-only (the launcher character-pick re-applies
saves onto the live world). Consequences fixed: save-scum duplication (stale
re-apply resurrected the kit AND kept the truck), cross-save vehicle leakage on
character switch, and two identically-parked vehicles collapsing to one on reload.
Fix: despawn all vehicles, respawn the saved set — the save decides, exactly like
inventory. The dead review agent had left a repro test asserting the bug; rewritten
as the regression lock `stale_reapply_rewinds_instead_of_duplicating`. Third
finding (creative deploys free vehicles) accepted by design.

## v0.679.0 — Stage 2: factory world-spawn

The Vehicle Assembler machine (home.ron catalog, `auto_recipe: assemble_rover`)
rolls REAL rovers onto the pad in front of it — drone → smelter → assembler is
mine-ore-to-vehicle untouched. Architecture call: extend CraftingSystem (the
battle-tested job engine with the Phase 1 hardening) rather than activate the
dormant ManufacturingSystem — one job engine, recipes stay in recipes.csv.
Machines gained a world Transform (first time). The pre-commit review confirmed
4 findings / 2 root causes, both fixed pre-ship: pad occupancy (12 world-queried
lanes; a FULL pad pauses the line with inputs unconsumed; freed lanes get reused)
and save-rewind duplication of in-flight batches (the apply site raises
`abort_active_crafts`; a rewind now behaves like an app restart).

## v0.680.0 — Stage 3 slice 1: vehicles MOVE

The first moving vehicle in HumanityOS. New `VehicleRoute` component ticked by
VehicleSystem on game time (straight-line travel, yaws to face motion, parks
within 4 m); per-vehicle speeds in kits.ron. The Inventory page gained a Vehicles
section — Summon a parked vehicle and it drives itself to you. Shipped WITHOUT the
usual pre-commit review (operator flagged ~28% budget; commit-early discipline
won) — the review is the logged fast-follow.

## v0.681.0 — field-test fixes (the operator played it)

First live field test of Stages 1–3 produced screenshots and three findings:
1. **Crew floating mid-sky** — the relay simulates chores on ITS multi-deck ship
   layout while the client renders the flat homestead. Client-side grounding fix
   at the sync source; the REAL fix (relay/client layout alignment) is tracked.
2. **The assembler said "assembling" with no percentage and produced nothing** —
   it had no rubber in stock and nothing said so. CraftingSystem now publishes a
   live status line per auto machine ("Assemble Rover — 42%", "waiting for Steel
   Ingot x6", "pad full, line paused"), shown in the Vehicles section.
3. **Drone dock pops on launch/return** — a real docking/undocking sequence is
   logged for the polish pass.
