The Simple Truth

All of us are family whether we like it or not. At the bare minimum each of us are genetically related as homo sapiens for the last million years.  Even further down the rabbit hole you’ll find we share DNA with most life on Earth. Technically we’re related to everything in the universe as star dust.

Project Universe becomes increasingly real as hours, days, years, decades are spent developing. Creating a video game capable of teaching everyone everything is an unfathomably difficult task made infinitely easier the more of us voluntarily working together to sculpt this masterpiece.
