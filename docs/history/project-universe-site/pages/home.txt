Welcome to Project Universe – Where Dreams Take Flight
I'm Shaostoul, or Michael Boisson, and I'm thrilled you're here. I've got a vision, a dream really, called Project Universe—a dream that's not mine alone but belongs to all of us who envision a brighter future. Together, we're on a mission to end global poverty, unite humanity, and prepare for our interplanetary destiny. If you believe in the power of open-source collaboration, you're already part of this journey.
 
What is Project Universe?
More than just a game, it's a revolutionary platform designed to empower each of us. Here, we'll teach:
STEM subjects

Practical Trades like farming, welding, building, and crafting

Life Skills including critical thinking and strategic planning

Imagine learning how to grow your own food, construct your home, or master skills for space living—all through an immersive, engaging gameplay experience. This isn't just about playing; it's about preparing for a future where education is accessible to everyone, anywhere—even from the comfort of a tiny bedroom aboard a spaceship.
Our Journey So Far
Since January 29th, 2019, we've been on this incredible journey. Progress might feel like moving through space itself—vast and slow at times—but every step forward is a leap towards our goal. Project Universe is designed to be:
Universally Accessible: Playable offline, online, or on private servers.

Inclusive: Available to all, regardless of your circumstances.

The Vision
Our core belief? Ending poverty is the key to uniting humanity. By keeping Project Universe open-source and in the public domain, we ensure it's driven by humanity's best interests, not corporate profit. You'll find no paywalls here; everything is free to use, modify, and enhance.
The Team
We might not have the big bucks for a large paid team, but we've got something better—a preposterous, passionate group of volunteer nerds dedicated to turning this dream into reality. Every contribution, whether it's your time, skills, or spreading the word, propels Project Universe forward, helping us reach a tech demo, attract more support, and make a tangible impact on billions of lives.
 
An Invitation to You
This isn't just an introduction; it's an invitation. Join me, join us, in making Project Universe a reality. Let's change the world, one player, one skill, one solar system at a time.
