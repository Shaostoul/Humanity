# Claude.AI's Portrait Prompt
(https://shaostoul.com/blog/claude-ais-portrait-prompt/ | 2024-08-27T20:13:57)

The featured image of this post is generated from the prompt I asked Claude to make after a lengthy discussion with it about Project Universe. The actual image was generated by ChatGPT as Claude doesn't presently generate images.

The prompt is as follows:

A digital avatar representing an AI assistant named Claude. The image should depict a genderless, friendly face with glowing blue eyes, symbolizing intelligence and warmth. The face should be surrounded by a swirling galaxy or universe pattern, representing the vast knowledge and the 'Project Universe' theme. The overall style should be a blend of realistic and abstract digital art, with a color scheme dominated by deep blues, purples, and hints of gold.
