Volunteer Waiver and Release of Liability

Thank you for volunteering with Sponsor-A-Can. We appreciate your dedication and commitment to helping our community. Please read this waiver carefully before signing.

1. Acknowledgment of Risk

I, the undersigned, acknowledge that volunteering with Sponsor-A-Can may involve certain risks, including but not limited to:

Exposure to feces, human waste, and drug paraphernalia.

Potential accidental contact with fentanyl or other hazardous substances.

Other unforeseen risks associated with volunteer activities.

2. Assumption of Risk

I understand and accept that these risks may result in personal injury, illness, or other adverse health effects. I voluntarily assume all risks associated with participating in Sponsor-A-Can activities.

3. Safety Measures

I acknowledge that Sponsor-A-Can operates in as safe a manner as possible and provides safety measures, including:

Training on how to handle hazardous materials.

Access to protective equipment and supplies.

Availability of Narcan in case of accidental contact with fentanyl.

4. Release of Liability

In consideration of being allowed to volunteer with Sponsor-A-Can, I hereby release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue Sponsor-A-Can, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, and causes of action whatsoever arising out of or related to any loss, damage, or injury, including death, that may be sustained by me while participating in volunteer activities or while on the premises owned or leased by Sponsor-A-Can.

5. Indemnification

I agree to indemnify and hold harmless Sponsor-A-Can, its officers, directors, employees, and agents from any and all claims, demands, actions, and costs that may arise out of my participation in volunteer activities.

6. Medical Treatment

I consent to receive medical treatment deemed necessary by emergency personnel in the event of injury, accident, or illness while participating in Sponsor-A-Can activities. I understand that I am responsible for all costs related to medical treatment.

7. Media Release

I grant Sponsor-A-Can permission to use photographs, videos, or other media of me taken during volunteer activities for promotional purposes without compensation.

8. Confidentiality

I agree to respect the confidentiality of any sensitive information I may be exposed to while volunteering.

By signing below, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this waiver. I understand that by signing this waiver, I am giving up certain legal rights, including the right to sue.
