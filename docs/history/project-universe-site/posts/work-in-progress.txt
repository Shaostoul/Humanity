# Work-In-Progress
(https://shaostoul.com/document/legal/work-in-progress/ | 2024-07-18T13:36:48)

Welcome to Project Universe!

Current Status:

Game Development: The game is not yet playable. We are diligently working on developing various features and functionalities, but it will take time before a playable version is available.

Website Functionality: The website is in its early stages and may not be fully operational. You may experience limited functionality as we continue to build and improve the site. My primary goal is to get the website made as it functions as the game design document, the hub for everything related to Project Universe, and the pitch deck.

Discord Community: Our Discord server is fully operational and serves as the main hub for volunteer developers to coordinate, share updates, and collaborate on the project. We encourage you to join us there for the latest news and to participate in the development process.

Volunteer Efforts: Development efforts are driven by voluntary contributions. As we are limited by the time we can commit, progress may vary. Your patience and understanding are greatly appreciated. We need significantly more volunteers and/or funding to be able to hire developers full-time for development to reach a usable state remotely soon.

Funding and Resources: Our funding is just enough to cover essential costs such as the website and other tools/services we use. We rely heavily on voluntary efforts to move the project forward.

What to Expect:

Ongoing Updates: Regular updates and new features will be added to enhance your experience. This includes improvements to the game, additional educational resources, and new functionalities on the website.

Feedback Integration: We value your feedback and encourage you to share your thoughts and suggestions. Your input helps us shape Project Universe to better meet the needs of our community.

Temporary Disruptions: There may be occasional disruptions or downtimes as we implement updates and improvements. We strive to minimize these interruptions and will provide advance notice whenever possible.

How You Can Help:

Report Issues: If you encounter any bugs or issues, please report them through our contact form or dedicated support channels.

Provide Feedback: Share your thoughts on existing features and suggestions for new ones.

Stay Engaged: Follow our updates and announcements to stay informed about the latest developments.

Volunteer: If you have skills or time to contribute, join us in our development efforts. Every bit helps, and your involvement can make a significant difference. No prior experience necessary to start!

Thank you for being part of this journey with us. Together, we are building a platform that aims to empower individuals, foster global collaboration, and create a better future for all.

For any inquiries or support, please contact us at contact@shaostoul.com.
