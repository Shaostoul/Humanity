# Accessibility Statement
(https://shaostoul.com/document/legal/accessibility-statement/ | 2024-07-17T18:56:22)

I am dedicated to making Shaostoul.com and Project Universe accessible to everyone. I believe that every individual, regardless of their abilities, should have free, easy access to tools that help them survive and improve their lives.

My Commitment

I am committed to ensuring that Shaostoul.com and Project Universe are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. My goal is to create a wholesome environment where everyone can participate and benefit from our efforts to end poverty and unite humanity.

Measures to Support Accessibility

To achieve our accessibility goals, I have implemented the following measures:

User-Friendly Design: Ensuring our website and content are designed to be user-friendly and accessible to individuals with diverse needs.

Standards Compliance: Striving to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, which provides a comprehensive standard for accessible web content.

Assistive Technologies: Testing our website with various assistive technologies to ensure compatibility and usability.

Continuous Improvement: Regularly reviewing and updating our accessibility practices to enhance the user experience for everyone.

Accessibility Features

Here are some of the accessibility features I have incorporated:

Keyboard Navigation: Our website is designed to be navigable using a keyboard, allowing users who cannot use a mouse to access all features and content.

Text Alternatives: Providing text alternatives for non-text content, such as images and videos, to ensure all users can access the information they convey.

Readable Content: Ensuring our text is readable and understandable, with sufficient contrast between text and background colors.

Scalable Text: Allowing users to adjust the text size to meet their needs without losing functionality or content.

Feedback and Assistance

Your feedback is invaluable in helping me improve accessibility. If you encounter any accessibility barriers or have suggestions on how I can improve, please let me know. I am committed to addressing any issues promptly and effectively.

Contact Information

For any questions, concerns, or feedback about accessibility, please contact me at [contact@shaostoul.com]. I am here to assist you and ensure your experience with Shaostoul.com and Project Universe is positive and inclusive.

Future Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing journey, and I am committed to continuous improvement. As technology and accessibility standards evolve, I will keep updating our website and practices to provide the best possible experience for all users.

Thank you for your support and for being part of our mission to create a better world. Together, we can make a positive impact and ensure Shaostoul.com and Project Universe are accessible to everyone.
