# Acceptable Use Policy
(https://shaostoul.com/document/legal/acceptable-use-policy/ | 2024-07-17T18:48:37)

Welcome to Shaostoul.com and Project Universe. This Acceptable Use Policy outlines the rules and guidelines for using our website and services. By accessing or using Shaostoul.com and Project Universe, you agree to comply with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

1. Purpose

The purpose of this Acceptable Use Policy is to ensure a safe, respectful, and productive environment for all users of Shaostoul.com and Project Universe.

2. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to use our services to:

Engage in Illegal Activities: Do not engage in any activities that are illegal, unlawful, or fraudulent.

Harm Others: Do not harass, abuse, harm, or otherwise violate the legal rights of others.

Distribute Malicious Software: Do not upload, distribute, or transmit viruses, malware, or any other harmful software.

Violate Privacy: Do not collect or store personal information about other users without their consent.

Impersonate Others: Do not impersonate any person or entity, or falsely state or otherwise misrepresent your affiliation with a person or entity.

Spam: Do not send unsolicited or unauthorized advertising, promotional materials, junk mail, spam, chain letters, or any other form of solicitation.

Interfere with Services: Do not interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of our services or the data contained therein.

Access Unauthorized Areas: Do not attempt to gain unauthorized access to any part of our website, accounts, computer systems, or networks.

3. User Responsibilities

Compliance: You are responsible for ensuring your use of our services complies with this policy and all applicable laws and regulations.

Content: You are responsible for any content you create, transmit, or display while using our services.

Security: You are responsible for maintaining the security and confidentiality of your account information, including your username and password.

4. Enforcement

We are committed to providing continuous access to our tools and resources, which are crucial for survival and the mission to end poverty and unite humanity. However, to maintain a safe and respectful environment, we may need to take action in cases of serious violations:

Monitor Use: We may monitor and review your use of our services to ensure compliance with this policy.

Remove Content: We may remove any content that violates this policy or is otherwise objectionable in our sole discretion.

Limited Access Restrictions: In cases of serious violations, we may restrict certain functionalities or features temporarily to address the issue while ensuring continued access to essential tools and resources.

Collaborative Resolution: We will work with you to resolve any issues and restore full access as soon as possible, ensuring that actions taken are in the best interest of both the individual and the community.

5. Reporting Violations

If you become aware of any violation of this policy, please report it to us immediately at [contact@shaostoul.com].

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Acceptable Use Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and it is your responsibility to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Acceptable Use Policy, please contact us at [contact@shaostoul.com].

Thank you for being a part of Shaostoul.com and Project Universe. Together, we can create a safe, respectful, and innovative community while ensuring access to tools that help everyone survive and thrive.
