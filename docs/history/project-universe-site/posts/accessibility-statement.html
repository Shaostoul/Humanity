Accessibility Statement

I am dedicated to making Shaostoul.com and Project Universe accessible to everyone. I believe that every individual, regardless of their abilities, should have free, easy access to tools that help them survive and improve their lives.

My Commitment

I am committed to ensuring that Shaostoul.com and Project Universe are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. My goal is to create a wholesome environment where everyone can participate and benefit from our efforts to end poverty and unite humanity.

Measures to Support Accessibility

To achieve our accessibility goals, I have implemented the following measures:

  1. User-Friendly Design: Ensuring our website and content are designed to be user-friendly and accessible to individuals with diverse needs.
  2. Standards Compliance: Striving to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA, which provides a comprehensive standard for accessible web content.
  3. Assistive Technologies: Testing our website with various assistive technologies to ensure compatibility and usability.
  4. Continuous Improvement: Regularly reviewing and updating our accessibility practices to enhance the user experience for everyone.

Accessibility Features

Here are some of the accessibility features I have incorporated:

  1. Keyboard Navigation: Our website is designed to be navigable using a keyboard, allowing users who cannot use a mouse to access all features and content.
  2. Text Alternatives: Providing text alternatives for non-text content, such as images and videos, to ensure all users can access the information they convey.
  3. Readable Content: Ensuring our text is readable and understandable, with sufficient contrast between text and background colors.
  4. Scalable Text: Allowing users to adjust the text size to meet their needs without losing functionality or content.
