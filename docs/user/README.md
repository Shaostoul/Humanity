# For users

This folder is for **people who want to use HumanityOS**. You do not need to know how
to code, run a server, or understand cryptography. You just want tools that help you
communicate, organize your life, learn real skills, and connect with a community, and
you want to actually own your data while you do it.

## Start here

- **[getting-started.md](getting-started.md)** the plain-language quickstart: what
  HumanityOS is, why it exists, and how to begin in a few minutes.
- **[ONBOARDING.md](ONBOARDING.md)** the fuller story: the two layers (the platform and
  the game), the Humanity Accord, and where the project stands.

## What you can do today

Chat in channels, send end-to-end encrypted direct messages, make voice and video
calls, follow and friend people, keep tasks and notes, browse a marketplace, track an
inventory, grow a garden in the simulation, and read the in-app Library.

## What makes it different

- **No accounts, no passwords.** Your identity is a key that lives on your device. You
  back it up with a 24-word phrase. Nobody can lock you out, and nobody can read your
  private messages, not even the people running the server.
- **You own your data.** No ads, no tracking, no selling you.
- **It is free and public-domain (CC0).** Use it, share it, change it, forever.
- **It works your way.** Run it in a browser, install the desktop app, or join any
  server you trust. Your identity travels with you.
