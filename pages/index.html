Why This Exists

What makes it different

🔑

You own your identity

Your identity is a cryptographic keypair stored in your browser. No server can take it away. Move to any server and take your keys with you.

🔐

Private by default

DMs are encrypted end-to-end. The server never sees plaintext. No IP logging. No analytics. Nothing stored that doesn't need to exist.

🌐

Federated

Anyone can run a server. Your identity works across all servers. No central authority controls access.

📜

Public domain

Everything here is CC0. No permission required. No attribution required. This belongs to everyone — present and future.

🧰

More than chat

Project boards, marketplace, asset library, skill tracking, maps, inventory. The full life-management OS, not just a chat app.

🎮

A game that teaches

Project Universe is a free game teaching practical skills — homesteading, agriculture, building, health. Your game progress reflects real capability.

The Mission

Poverty is lack of capability

Not just lack of money. People trapped in systems they can't understand, knowledge they can't access, skills they never learned.

In 2017, Michael Boisson nearly died. That experience stripped away the noise and left one clear answer: help people become capable of helping themselves. Education is the foundation. Tools are the path. Community is the engine.

Everything here is public domain — CC0. No permission needed. This belongs to everyone.
