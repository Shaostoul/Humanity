Your dwelling. Rooms you can furnish, manage, and expand. Each room serves a purpose -- from growing food to managing power.
Room-based home management with localStorage persistence (key: hos_home_v1). Seven default rooms covering living, sleep, food, growing, crafting, storage, and utilities. Garden room features aeroponic tower visualization (50 plant slots, spiral layout) plus traditional soil beds. Power & Water room merges the old Systems page: solar, batteries, water filtration, network, and environmental sensors. Rooms can be added/removed/reordered by the user.
