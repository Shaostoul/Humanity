What is this?

A shared toolkit for working together

HumanityOS is for people who want to work together on anything: a neighborhood project, a small business, a study group, a worldwide movement. It puts messaging, task boards, a marketplace, notes, a calendar, and a digital wallet into one app that anyone can run, nobody can shut down, and everyone owns.

7 years in. 150+ features live. Completely in the public domain.

What you can do today

Five things, not a list of parts

Talk to anyone, privately.

Text, voice, and video calls. Messages are locked with math only the people in the conversation can unlock. Screen share, threads, search, reactions.

Organize any project.

Kanban boards, calendars, shared notes, skills tracking. Run a team, a club, or your own life from one place.

Buy, sell, and tip.

A built-in marketplace with listings and reviews, plus a Solana wallet for payments, staking, and NFTs. Your identity is your wallet.

Work offline, own your data.

Install the desktop app and everything keeps working without internet. When you reconnect, it syncs.

Run your own copy.

One small program is the app, the server, and the game engine. Host it for your community, or join the shared network.

Live right now at united-humanity.us/chat. 150+ features shipped. Every line on GitHub. No corporation, no VCs. Built in the open since 2019.

Why This Exists

What makes it different

You own your identity

No username-and-password account a company can lock or delete. A single key, stored on your device, is your login everywhere. Back it up with a short word-list.

Encrypted by default

Private messages are scrambled on your device before they leave. The server never sees what you said. No ads, no tracking, no IP logs.

No central owner

Anyone can run a server. They all talk to each other. If one goes down, the network keeps working.

Works offline

The desktop app keeps running without internet. Catches up when you reconnect.

A 3D world, built in

A simulated universe for learning, playing, and prototyping: planets, farming, crafting, construction. Early, but already renders and runs.

Public domain

No license fee, no attribution required, no permission to ask. Copy it, sell it, teach from it. It belongs to everyone.

Still following?

Still not sure what this is?

Think of it as Discord, Slack, Trello, Etsy, and a Google-Docs-style offline notebook, rolled into one app, with a video-game world attached. No company owns any of it. You run it from your phone, laptop, or your own server. Your name and your wallet are the same thing: one key on your device.

The mission is simple. Poverty is mostly a shortage of tools, knowledge, and trusted community. So we are building those, for free, forever.

The Mission

Poverty is lack of capability

Not just lack of money. People trapped in systems they can't understand, knowledge they can't reach, skills they never had a chance to learn.

In 2017, Michael Boisson nearly died. That experience stripped away the noise and left one answer: help people become capable of helping themselves. Education is the foundation. Tools are the path. Community is the engine.

Everything here is public domain. No permission needed. This belongs to everyone.

For Developers

The technical stack

Server: Rust / axum / tokio relay. SQLite storage. Federated server-to-server WebSocket relay.
Identity: Ed25519 self-custody keys double as Solana wallet addresses. BIP39 seed backup and key rotation.
Crypto: ECDH + AES-256-GCM for E2E DMs. WebRTC voice and video.
Client: Plain JS frontend, no build step. Native desktop app with wgpu + WGSL 3D renderer.
License: CC0 public domain, every commit on GitHub.
