Your digital wallet for sending, receiving, and managing crypto on Solana.
Self-custody Solana wallet derived from your Ed25519 identity key. Supports SOL, SPL tokens (USDC), swaps via Jupiter, native staking, and NFTs. All signing happens client-side; private keys never leave the device.

Manage your Solana wallet. Send and receive SOL and tokens, swap between assets, stake for rewards, and view your NFTs.

Your identity key doubles as your Solana wallet — no extra setup needed.
