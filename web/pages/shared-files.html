Shared Files

Real, useful files shared by people on this server: 3D-printable parts, models, and designs. Download them, print them, improve them, share them back.

How to share your own: open the chat, click the attach button, and pick your file. 3D and model files (.blend, .stl, .obj, .gltf, .glb) are listed here automatically; photos and other chat attachments stay private to the people you send them to. Sharing requires a verified account (ask an admin in chat).
