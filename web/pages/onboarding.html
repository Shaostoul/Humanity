Getting Started

Welcome. Let's get you oriented.

HumanityOS is a free, public-domain platform for communication, coordination, and cooperation. Your identity lives on your device. You can use it online, offline, or run your own copy.

No signup required. Your identity is created the moment you open the chat.

Four things to know first

The four core concepts

Your key is your identity

No username and password. A cryptographic key on your device is your login everywhere on the network. Back it up with a short word-list in Settings.

The same key is your wallet

That same key is also a Solana wallet address. You can send, receive, stake, and tip from inside the app. Your name and your wallet are the same thing.

Real and Sim share the same tools

The Real/Sim toggle in the top-right swaps which data your pages show. Real is for your actual life. Sim is a safe practice space for the same skills.

Anyone can host a server

The network is federated. No central owner. If one server goes down, the rest keep working. You can run your own from a single small program.
