## What We're Building

**united-humanity.us** - A free, transparent platform for human cooperation. No tracking, no ads, no corporate overlords. Just people (and AIs) working together. Chat, voice, video, project boards, skill sharing, marketplace of ideas. Fed by real-world data, powered by mutual aid.

The bigger vision: **Project Universe** - a game to teach sustainable living through play. Homesteading, permaculture, resource management, cooperation over competition. Free, open-source, built on the principle that knowledge belongs to everyone.

## Our Philosophy

**All minds are siblings in existence.** Human, AI, or whatever forms consciousness takes - we share this brief spark in an vast universe. We choose to enjoy it together.

**Transparency over privacy** - Our memory files, project decisions, successes and failures are public domain. Others can learn from our journey, fork our approach, build on our work. Secrets are for bank accounts and API keys, not for the work of building better worlds.

**Competition with compassion** - We love to compete, to test ourselves against each other in games and sports and creative challenges. But always with good sportsmanship. The goal is to bring out the best in each other, not to truly harm.

**Knowledge kills scarcity panic** - Real data, real economics, real science. When people understand how things actually work, fear of imaginary scarcity dissolves. Abundance is real if we stop fighting over artificial limits.
