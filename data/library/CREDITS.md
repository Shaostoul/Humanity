If you contributed and are missing, tell us - being remembered is the point.

## Data and imagery sources

- **NASA Blue Marble Next Generation** (Earth surface imagery, Aug 2004
  composite) - NASA Earth Observatory. Public domain.
- **NOAA/NCEI ETOPO** global relief (terrain + bathymetry tiles) - public
  domain.
- **NASA GIBS / MODIS** live cloud-fraction imagery (the in-game live
  weather) - public domain.
- **ESA Gaia DR3** and the **AT-HYG** star catalog compilation (the night
  sky's 25M-star bake) - ESA/Gaia/DPAC (CC-BY-SA-IGO 3.0) and AT-HYG
  (CC-BY-SA / public-domain components).
- **Solar System Scope textures** (Moon, Mars planetary imagery bakes) -
  CC-BY 4.0, https://www.solarsystemscope.com/textures/ - based on NASA
  elevation and imagery data.
- **NASA New Horizons** Pluto global color map - NASA/JHUAPL/SwRI. Public
  domain.
- **USGS Astrogeology** planetary mosaics (reference + future bakes) -
  public domain.
