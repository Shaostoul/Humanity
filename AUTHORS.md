AUTHORS.md

This work was initiated by Michael Patrick Boisson.

It began as an attempt to explore how humans might live together peacefully, scale cooperation without domination, and preserve dignity, knowledge, and continuity over long horizons.

The initiator has intentionally released this work into the public domain so that no individual, organization, or era may claim ownership over it.

Authorship here records provenance, not authority.

This file exists so future readers may know that this work began with a human choice to give it away.
